This is the Portfolio of Gray, I work for Animation. I work in the styles of either frame by frame or rigging.

There is a latest show I am doing. (Not my art). The series is about a high school girl having a normal, busy day until she fell down into from a hill and accidentally hit a tombstone and set a spirit free. She got mad and now controls and possesses her. She hated it until knowing the story of a recent evil spirit possessing a CEO man, can she stop him or will be too much for her?

Here is some collection of arts. I started to do art since middle school when I felt bored at home and decided to take a night class of art and I loved making art. It was fun having your creative brain and using it to make art.

I do all kind of art and love to experiment on art. Personally, I am a fan of doing something different in art each time I do something new to make it feel like something is new and fresh to me for art.
